read some books about mindfulness

A general good place to start is with any introductory or beginners book. This may seem obvious but it’s good to remind ourselves that we won’t become advanced overnight!

  • Great instroductory books include “Mindfulness: A practical guide to finding peace in a frantic world” by Prof. Mark Williams and Dr. Danny Penman.

  • For a read about integrating mindfulness into daily life, we’d recommend “This is happening” by Rohan Gunatillake.

  • Wanting an overview of the science? Check out “The Science of Meditation” by Daniel Goleman and Richard Davidson.

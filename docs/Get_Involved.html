Get Involved


Interested in getting involved?

Please come along to one of our information sessions!


Information Sessions

Informal information sessions will give you the oppunity to learn more about mindfulness, how it can be applied to student life, what apps to use and more about our project. Sessions will conclude with a guided meditation and ample time to ask any questions. Please feel free to stay for as little or as long as you want.

Information sessions will take place on Mondays, 4-5pm.

November 19th - Sir Alwyn Williams (SAW) building, 422

December 3rd - SAW, 422

December 9th - SAW, 423


Mini-Information Sessions

If you cannot make it to the larger sessions, smaller sessions will also be taking place at various times to give you the opportunity to pop by and learn more about the project and have a mindful break.

Please book a time.
